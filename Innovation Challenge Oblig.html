Problem

Overflow of garbage is a steady increasing problem in today's world society. As population grows, so does the number of consumers. With a increasing number consumers so does the quantity of garbage thrown everyday. As a result to this, we've come up with a long term solution as of how to streamline the way we throw garbage in Oslo.

Solution

Our solution to the problem is a new, smart trashcan. With sensor technology, we can track the amount of garbage thrown, type of garbage and it's worth in money.
